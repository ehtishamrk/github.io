Business assets depreciate on paper but are never revalued. Property held for years without any yield analysis. Equipment maintained based on habit, not cost-benefit. What We Do

How AOLFS Delivers This Service

📋

Asset Inventory & Valuation

A complete inventory of every business and personal asset — physical, financial, and intangible — with current market valuations, book values, useful life assessments, and yield or return calculations. Most owners discover significant value they weren't tracking.

📊

Portfolio Strategy & Allocation

Analysis of your current asset mix against your risk tolerance, liquidity needs, and financial objectives — with a recommended allocation strategy that balances return, risk, and liquidity across your total portfolio.

🏗️

Business Asset Optimisation

For each business asset: is it being used at optimal capacity? Is it generating appropriate return? Should it be upgraded, maintained, sold, or repurposed? We answer these questions with data, not intuition.

📈

Investment Planning

For liquid assets and investable capital: structured investment planning within Pakistan's available investment vehicles — government securities, term deposits, mutual funds, real estate, and equity — calibrated to your return objectives and risk profile.

⚠️

Risk Assessment & Insurance Review

Identification of uninsured or underinsured asset risks, review of existing insurance coverage for adequacy, and recommendations for risk mitigation that protect asset value against foreseeable threats.

📉

Asset Disposal & Recycling

For underperforming or redundant assets: disposal strategy, valuation for sale, buyer identification where applicable, and reinvestment planning for released capital — so asset recycling generates maximum value rather than fire-sale outcomes.
