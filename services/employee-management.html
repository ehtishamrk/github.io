System Modules

What the System Covers

Three core module groups — each solving a distinct management problem, all sharing one employee record.

Module 01

📅 Time & Attendance

  • Biometric or app-based clock-in/out
  • Real-time attendance dashboard
  • Late arrival and early departure tracking
  • Overtime calculation and approval workflow
  • Shift scheduling and roster management
  • Monthly attendance reports per employee
  • Direct payroll integration — no manual re-entry
Module 02

🏖️ Leave Management

  • Annual, sick, casual, and maternity leave tracking
  • Digital leave request and approval workflow
  • Leave balance visibility for every employee
  • Leave encashment calculation
  • Manager coverage and team calendar view
  • Pakistan Labour Law entitlement compliance
  • Year-end leave carry-forward rules
Module 03

📋 Employee Records & Lifecycle

  • Complete employee profile (personal, employment, documents)
  • Contract and CNIC document storage
  • Probation tracking and confirmation workflow
  • Disciplinary action records with full audit trail
  • Performance review history per employee
  • Salary revision and promotion history
  • Resignation, clearance, and separation management
