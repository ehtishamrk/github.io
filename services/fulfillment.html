⚠ The Problem

Pakistan's E-Commerce Growth Is Being Undermined by Fulfillment Failures That Drive Away the Customers the Marketing Budget Paid to Acquire.

Customer acquisition cost in Pakistani e-commerce is rising. Return rates are running at 20–35% on many platforms. Negative reviews citing late delivery, wrong items, and damaged goods are multiplying. And most online sellers are running fulfillment from a bedroom, a back room, or a WhatsApp group — with no tracking system, no quality control, and no returns process that makes sense. The product might be excellent. The marketing might be working. But without a proper fulfillment operation, you are spending money to acquire customers you are then losing through the experience of receiving the order.

End-to-End View

Every Stage of the Order Journey, Optimised

AOLFS designs and implements every stage of your fulfillment operation — from the moment an order is placed to the moment it's delivered, and back again if it's returned.

Stage 01
Order Receipt & Processing

Order management system captures every order from every channel — website, Daraz, social media, WhatsApp — in one dashboard. Orders are auto-verified, fraud-flagged, and queued for picking within minutes of placement.

Target: <15 min order-to-pick
Stage 02
Warehousing & Inventory

Warehouse layout optimised for pick speed. SKU locations mapped digitally. Real-time stock levels prevent overselling. Daily cycle counts prevent inventory drift. Receiving process ensures every inbound shipment is verified and logged.

Target: 99%+ inventory accuracy
Stage 03
Pick, Pack & Quality Check

Standardised picking workflow, packing station setup, packaging specification per SKU, and a mandatory quality check before seal — reducing wrong-item and damaged-on-arrival rates to near zero.

Target: <0.5% error rate
Stage 04
Dispatch & Last-Mile

Courier selection and rate benchmarking. Dispatch SOP ensuring same-day or next-day handover. Tracking number communication to customer within 2 hours of dispatch. Courier performance monitoring — on-time, damage, and loss rates tracked weekly.

Target: 95%+ on-time delivery
Stage 05
Returns Management

Clear return policy communicated at purchase. Digital returns request process. Return receipt and condition inspection. Restocking or write-off decision workflow. Refund or replacement processing within defined SLA. Return cost analysis to identify product and process issues.

Target: <48hr return resolution
