Pakistan Payroll Compliance Map

Every statutory payroll obligation your business carries — and what AOLFS does with each one.

Obligation Applicable To Frequency AOLFS Action
EOBI Contribution
Mandatory		 All employees ≥ PKR minimum wage Monthly Calculate (5% employer + 1% employee), generate challan, remit by 15th of following month
PESSI / SESSI
Mandatory		 Applicable by province and employee count threshold Monthly Register, calculate contributions, file monthly return, maintain employee records
FBR Salary Tax (WHT)
Mandatory		 Employees above tax-exempt threshold Monthly deposit, Annual statement Calculate progressive tax per FBR slab, deduct at source, deposit via CPR, issue annual certificate
Minimum Wage Compliance
Mandatory		 All employees — province-specific rates Ongoing Monitor provincial minimum wage updates, flag non-compliant salary bands, update payroll accordingly
Gratuity Provision
As applicable		 Employees with 5+ years of service On termination / Annual accrual Calculate monthly provision, maintain gratuity ledger, compute final payment on separation
Bonus Provisions
As applicable		 As per employment contract or Ordinance Annually / as triggered Calculate Eid bonuses and profit participation, maintain provisions, process on schedule
