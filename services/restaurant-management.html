How We Work

Our Process

Every AOLFS engagement follows the same rigorous methodology — adapted to your specific context, scale, and industry.

01

Operations Audit

We spend time in your restaurant across multiple shifts — observing every process, measuring waste, documenting the current flow, and quantifying where money is leaking.

02

System Design

We select the right tools, configure your menu and recipes, set up inventory parameters, and design your reporting framework.

03

Implementation

Weekly reviews for the first month. We track your daily P&L, identify remaining leakage, and optimize until your margins are where they should be.
