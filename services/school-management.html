⚠ The Problem

The Average Pakistani School Loses 8–15% of Fee Revenue Every Year to Collection Gaps, Waiver Abuse, and Unrecorded Admissions.

Fee challans issued on paper with no digital record. Students attending whose fee status nobody can confirm quickly. Concessions and waivers approved verbally with no documentation trail. Teachers marked present in registers that nobody audits. Results compiled manually in Excel that takes two weeks to produce at exam time. Parent complaints that go nowhere because there's no formal communication channel. A new student admitted with no proper documentation. These are not exceptional problems in Pakistani schools — they are the norm. And they represent both revenue loss and institutional risk that a proper school management system eliminates completely.
