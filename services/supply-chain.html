What We Build

Six Supply Chain Capabilities We Deliver

🏭

Vendor Selection & Qualification

Market mapping, RFQ process, vendor evaluation framework, and approved vendor list — establishing 2–3 qualified suppliers per category so your business is never dependent on a single source or subject to monopoly pricing.

📋

Procurement System & Controls

Digital purchase order system with approval hierarchy, budget limits, and three-way matching (PO, GRN, invoice) — so every rupee spent on procurement is authorised, verified, and reconciled. No more verbal orders and disputed invoices.

📦

Inventory Optimisation

Reorder point calculation, Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) modelling, and safety stock levels calibrated to your demand patterns and lead times — eliminating both stockouts and the working capital cost of excess inventory.

🚚

Logistics Architecture

Carrier selection, rate benchmarking, route optimisation, delivery SLA definition, and claims management framework — reducing logistics cost and improving delivery reliability simultaneously.

📊

Vendor Performance Management

Monthly vendor scorecards measuring on-time delivery, quality rejection rate, pricing compliance, and responsiveness — with structured review meetings and consequence frameworks that keep suppliers performing at contracted standards.

📉

Cost Reduction Programme

A systematic programme identifying the top 10 cost reduction opportunities in your supply chain — through renegotiation, consolidation, specification optimisation, and process improvement — with a trackable savings target and timeline.
